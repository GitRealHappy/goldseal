## Professional Heating Services

Stay comfortable year-round with Gold Seal Mechanical's comprehensive heating services. Our licensed technicians specialize in all types of heating systems, from traditional furnaces to modern heat pumps, serving the Fraser Valley and Greater Vancouver area.

### Our Heating Services Include:

#### Furnace Services
- Gas and electric furnace installation
- Furnace repair and troubleshooting
- Annual maintenance and tune-ups
- Filter replacement and cleaning
- Efficiency upgrades and retrofits

#### Boiler Services
- Boiler installation and replacement
- Boiler repair and maintenance
- Hydronic heating systems
- Radiant floor heating
- Steam and hot water systems

#### Heat Pump Services
- Air source heat pump installation
- Ground source (geothermal) heat pumps
- Ductless mini-split systems
- Heat pump repair and maintenance
- Energy-efficient upgrades

- Annual heating system inspections
- Preventive maintenance programs
- Duct cleaning and sealing
- Thermostat installation and programming
- Energy efficiency assessments

#### Emergency Heating Repairs
- 24/7 emergency heating service
- No heat emergency calls
- System breakdowns and failures
- Carbon monoxide testing
- Safety inspections
